# Missing SEO Assets

Please add the following files to the `/public` directory:

## Required Images

1. **apple-touch-icon.png** (180x180px)
   - iOS home screen icon
   - Should be your logo/brand mark
   - PNG format, 180x180 pixels

2. **og-image.png** (1200x630px)
   - Open Graph image for social media sharing
   - Displays when your portfolio is shared on Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter
   - Should include your name and title
   - PNG format, 1200x630 pixels
   - Recommended: Use Canva or Figma to create

3. **favicon.ico** (optional, but recommended)
   - Traditional favicon format
   - 32x32 or 16x16 pixels
   - ICO format

## Quick Creation Tips

### For og-image.png:
- Background: Dark (#0F0E0E)
- Text: "Rameshwar Bhagwat | Full Stack & AI Developer"
- Include: Your photo or logo
- Add: Tech stack icons (React, Next.js, TypeScript, etc.)

### For apple-touch-icon.png:
- Use your logo or initials "RB"
- Simple, recognizable design
- Works well at small sizes

## Tools to Create:
- Canva (free templates)
- Figma
- Photoshop
- Online favicon generators

Once created, place these files in the `/public` directory.
